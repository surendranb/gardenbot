Sentinel-1 Wiring Guide

Arduino Uno  •  BME680 (6-Pin Module)  •  4-Wire I2C

BME680 (6-PIN)
VIN → 5V ●
GND → GND ●
SCL → A5 ●
SDA → A4 ●
SDO (Floating) ○
CS (Floating) ○
ARDUINO UNO ANALOG RAIL
A0A1A2A3A4A5
WARNING: On Arduino Uno, I2C is HARD-HARDWIRED to Pins A4 and A5. Even if you have other analog sensors, these two MUST stay clear for the BME680 to respond.

Uno Pinout Mapping

  • Pin A4 BME680 SDA (Purple)
  • Pin A5 BME680 SCL (Yellow)
  • Pin A0 Soil P1
  • Pin A1 Light Sensor
  • Pin A2 Soil P3
  • Pin A3 Soil P2 (Mint)

The "Zero" Problem

If you are seeing `0.00` values, the I2C bus is likely failing to initialize. Ensure the BME680 is receiving 5V/3.3V and that GND is shared between the Uno and the Breadboard.

Address Hack: If SDO is left floating, try tying SDO to GND to force address 0x76.
